Supported Skin Conditions

Acne
Inflammatory skin condition with clogged pores, pimples, and cysts. Common in teenagers and young adults.
Hyperpigmentation
Darkening of skin due to excess melanin production. Often caused by sun exposure or hormonal changes.
Nail Psoriasis
Autoimmune condition affecting nails, causing pitting, discoloration, and nail separation.
Stevens-Johnson Syndrome
Rare but serious skin reaction, often to medications. Requires immediate medical attention.
Vitiligo
Autoimmune condition causing loss of skin pigmentation, resulting in white patches on the skin.
